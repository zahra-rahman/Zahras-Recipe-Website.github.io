Vanilla Cake

Serve with jam and cream for a light victoria sponge

Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 225g butter at rtp (with extra to grease tins)
  • Jam and double cream to serve
    • Recipie

    1. Preheat the oven to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4. Grease and line two 20cm/8in sandwich tins
    2. Break the eggs into a large mixing bowl, then add the sugar, flour, baking powder, vanilla extract and butter
      Mix together until well combined with an electric hand mixer until mixture is light and airy
    3. Divide the mixture evenly between the tins: this doesn’t need to be exact
    4. Bake the cakes on the middle shelf of the oven for 25 minutes. Check them after 20 minutes. The cakes are done when they’re golden-brown and coming away from the edge of the tins. Press them gently to check – they should be springy to the touch
    5. To assemble the cake, place one cake upside down onto a plate and spread it with plenty of jam. If you want to, you can spread over whipped cream too
