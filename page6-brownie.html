CHOCOLATE BROWNIES

SOFT AND CHOCOLATEY

Ingredients
  • 100g self rasising flour
  • 375g caster sugar
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 275g margarine
    • Recipie

    1. Cut a rectangle of non-stick baking paper to fit the base of a tin
      Grease the tin and preheat the oven to 180 degrees celcius
    2. Measure all the ingredients into a large bowl and whisk until evenly blended
    3. Spoon into a baking tray
    4. Here you can add extra chocolate or toppings like oreos that will bake into the brownies
    5. Bake for 45 mins until brownies have a crusty top and a skewer inserted comes out clean
