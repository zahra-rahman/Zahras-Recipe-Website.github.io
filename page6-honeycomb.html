HONEYCOMB

SWEET, CRISPY HONEYCOMB

Ingredients
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 5 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
    • Recipie

    1. mix sugar and golden syrup in a deep saucepan and stir over a gentle heat until the sugar has melted
    2. Once completely melted, turn up the heat a little and simmer until you have an amber coloured caramel
    3. As quickly as you can turn off the heat, tip in 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda and beat in with a wooden spoon until it has all disappeared and the mixture is foaming
    4. Scrape into the tin immediately but be careful as the mixture will be very hot
    5. The mixture will continue bubbling in the tin, simply leave it and in about 1 hr - 1 hr 30 mins the honeycomb will be hard and ready to crumble or snap into chunks.
