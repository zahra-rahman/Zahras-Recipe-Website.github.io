SWEETCORN CHICKEN

Delicious and simple chicken and sweetcorn curry

Ingredients
  • 1 red onion; finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic; crushed
  • handful of corriander; finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • chilli flakes
  • about 350g of sweetcorn
  • 500g chicken sliced
  • handful of cherry tomatoes
  • green pepper
  • 1 lime; zest and juice
  • pinch of salt
    • Recipie

    1. Put garlic, salt, onion, chilli, lime, corriander and sweetcorn into a bowl and stir
    2. Add the chicken in and leave to mariade
    3. Cook in a pan with oil until golden brown and add tomatoes, peppers and sweet corn
