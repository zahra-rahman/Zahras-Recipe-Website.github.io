COCONUT, BROCCOLI CHICKEN

Aromatic and flavoursome

Ingredients
  • 500g chicken breast
  • tin of canned coconut milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • lemon juice
  • 1 orange pepper
  • 1 broccoli
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • handful of corriander
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
    • Recipie

    1. Add olive oil and onions in a pan and mix until golden
    2. Add salt, curry powder, sugar, lemon kuice and a splash of water and mix until water evaporates
    3. Add coconut milk and stir for 3-5 mins
    4. Stir in chicken breast and leave for 5-10 mins until almost cooked
    5. Add pepepr and broccoli to cook (as well as any other veggie desired)
    6. Mix in garlic and corriander and serve with rice noodles or rotis
